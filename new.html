ARMY

The Indian Army is established on 1st April 1895.The Indian Army is the world's largest-standing all-volunteer Army, comprising over 1.2 million active troops and 0.9 million reserve troops. Indian Army is a great platform to showcase your leadership skills and patriotism. The Indian Army is the land-based branch of the Indian Armed Forces.

There are many ways to join the Indian Army.
Some best ways to join The Indian Army are
After 12th
1.)NDA(National Defence Academy) Examination
2.)TES(Technical Entry Scheme Course)
After Graduation
1.)CDS(Combined Defence Services) Examination

NAVY

The Indian Navy is established on 26th January 1950.The Indian Navy is currently the world's seventh most powerful marine force. The Indian Navy is the maritime branch of the Indian Armed Forces. Like other armed forces, The Indian Navy also offers you an oppurtunity to prove yourself as a leader and your love to your Nation.

There are many ways to join the Indian Navy.
Some best ways to join The Indian Navy are
After 12th

2.)INA(Indian Naval Academy) Entry
After Graduation
1.)CDS(Combined Defence Services)
